Welcome To AICTE


The All India Council for Technical Education
(AICTE) is a premier national body established by an Act of Parliament to
ensure the coordinated development and quality assurance of technical
education in India. Founded in 1945, AICTE has been instrumental in
fostering excellence in engineering, technology, management, and other
allied disciplines.
